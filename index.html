AFRICAN DICHES

Beef in Stew

A hearty dish made with tender beef chunks simmered in a rich, flavorful tomato-based sauce, often served with rice or fufu.

Beef Suya

A popular West African street food, Suya consists of thinly sliced beef marinated in a spicy peanut sauce, skewered, and grilled to perfection. It's often served with sliced onions, tomatoes, and cucumbers.

Beermate

A refreshing and tangy drink made from fermented millet or sorghum, often enjoyed as a traditional beverage in various African cultures.

Edikanikong

A rich and flavorful Nigerian soup made with a variety of meats, fish, and leafy vegetables, typically served with fufu or pounded yam.

Vegetable Ratatouille

A vibrant and colorful dish made with a medley of fresh vegetables such as eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes, cooked in a savory tomato sauce and seasoned with herbs.

Grilled Tiger Prawns

Succulent tiger prawns marinated in a blend of spices and herbs, then grilled to perfection, often served with a side of spicy dipping sauce.

Nkwobi

A traditional Nigerian delicacy made from cow foot cooked in a spicy palm oil sauce, garnished with utazi leaves and served in a wooden bowl.

Pepper Soup Assorted

A spicy and aromatic soup made with a variety of meats or fish, flavored with traditional African spices and herbs, often served as a comforting dish during cold weather or special occasions.

Vegetable Soup

A nutritious and flavorful soup made with a variety of fresh vegetables, often combined with meat or fish, and seasoned with traditional African spices.
